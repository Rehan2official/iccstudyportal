Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a resource?

Navigate to the "Upload Center" page using the main menu. Fill in the details about the resource (Semester, Subject, Resource Type) and select the file you wish to upload. Click the "Upload File" button. Our team will review the submission before it appears on the site.

How are resources verified?

Our team, composed of experienced students and faculty advisors, reviews each submission for relevance to the Osmania University syllabus, accuracy, and overall quality. We strive to ensure all shared resources are reliable and helpful.

Can I request specific resources?

Yes! The best way to request specific notes or materials is by asking in our official WhatsApp group. You can join the group via the link on the "Join Us" page. This allows other students who might have the resource to share it directly.

Who runs the ICC Resource Hub?

The ICC Resource Hub is a student-led initiative founded by Mohammad Abdul Rehan, Ismail Ahmed Khan, and Mohammed Zaid Omer. We are passionate about creating a centralized, reliable platform for OU commerce students.
